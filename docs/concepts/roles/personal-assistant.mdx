## Personal Assistant  

The **MAOTO Personal Assistant** is a specialized **[Provider](./provider)** built and maintained by **MAOTO** as a service to the user.  

### **Role of the Personal Assistant**  
- Acts as an **interface** between users and the **MAOTO Marketplace**.  
- Helps **translate user intent** into actionable tasks.  
- Manages **task structuring, posting, and tracking** on behalf of the user.  
- Abstracts away **complex interactions** between the **user, Providers, and Solvers**.  

By leveraging the **MAOTO Personal Assistant**, users can efficiently delegate tasks without needing direct interaction with the marketplace.
